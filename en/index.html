Pierluigi Monticone, known as Gigi Montico

Painting as form,
space and civic consciousness.

Born in Malnate (1943) and based in Dovera, Montico's career moves from figurative palette-knife painting to spatial constructions, material collage, and works now discussed through sustainability and Terminal Realism.

Explore artistic periods

Critical framework

Spatialized figuration

In the source books, color, movement, space and time are repeatedly described as a single pictorial unit, especially in works such as Sabrina and Milano.

Material ethics

Recovered matter is not decorative. It structures the image and grounds Montico's ecological and social stance across decades.
